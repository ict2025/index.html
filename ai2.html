Introduction to AI

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a wide-ranging branch of computer science concerned with building smart machines capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence.

History

The history of artificial intelligence (AI) can be traced back to ancient myths and legends, but the field began to take shape in the mid-20th century.

Benefits

Artificial intelligence (AI) has many benefits, including improved efficiency, reduced errors, and enhanced decision-making. AI can also help with complex tasks and global challenges.

Uses

Artificial intelligence (AI) is used in many fields, including healthcare, transportation, and education. AI uses algorithms and computer systems to learn and solve problems.
